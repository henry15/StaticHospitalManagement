Services

Hello, this is Shree Krishna Hospital. We have best services for our patients and manage them.

Emergency Care

The Emergency Department at Doctors Hospital and Medical Center provides care to patients with urgent medical conditions ranging from complex problems. Care is provided 24 hours a day.

Call Center 24/7

Our agents are on hand 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and 365 days a year. There is always a courteous, live-voice representative ready to assist your patients and their families, whatever their needs may be.

Ambulance

Transfers from different hospitals for seriously ill patients requiring ventilator and monitor support. Transport of patients from home to the hospital. Transport of patients from hospital to home.
